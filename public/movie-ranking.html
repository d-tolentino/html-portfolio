Danielle's Favorite Movies

My top 5 movies:

  1. IronMan - This movie is action packed with entertainment if you like superheroes

    • After being held captive in an Afghan cave, billionaire engineer Tony Stark creates a unique weaponized suit of armor to fight evil.

  2. Avengers: Endgame - Entertaining and great ending to the Marvel series

    • After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War (2018), the universe is in ruins. With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe.

  3. Love and Monsters - This movie is weird but fun to watch

    • Seven years after he survived the monster apocalypse, lovably hapless Joel leaves his cozy underground bunker behind on a quest to reunite with his high school sweetheart.

  4. The Greatest Showman - Musical with an amazing take on how the circus came to be

    • Celebrates the birth of show business and tells of a visionary who rose from nothing to create a spectacle that became a worldwide sensation.

  5. Cruella - Great music in this film

    • Description: During the 1970s London punk rock revolution, young grifter Estella transforms herself into the raucous, revenge-bent Cruella de Vil.
